Data Science: a Complete Project

About this blog

The aim of this blog is to help readers form a mental model of Data Science. There are tons of material on the interent on languages, algorithms, data sets, etc. In my own experience, what's missing is to see how all these parts fit together. That is exactly the niche, this blog aims at: distill the central ideas and provide a complete picture of what Data Science is. You can find more at the About section

Instructions of use

Articles follow a time progression from the simplest to the more complicated. Every article has links to the, conceptually, previous and next articles. Articles come in conceptual groups, such as introduction, and these groups are represented in the categories. In this way, through the Categories navigation button, every article is two clicks away. At the same time, tags cut through categories, along certain concepts, such as statistics. The blog logo, on the left of the navigation manu, brings you back to this page.
